Programs & Services

Practical training designed to bridge the gap between potential and employment

We offer practical, hands-on workshops and one-on-one coaching designed to bridge the gap between potential and employment. Our programs are tailored to meet the needs of the youth & Adults in the Lowveld region.

Employability & Life Skills Workshops

Comprehensive training in communication, teamwork, problem-solving, time management, and other essential workplace skills.

CV Writing & Optimization

Learn to create professional, targeted resumes that stand out to employers. We cover formatting, content, and tailoring for specific industries.

Interview Preparation & Coaching

Mock interviews, common question practice, body language guidance, and techniques to build confidence for job interviews.

Job Application Assistance

Step-by-step guidance on completing job applications, writing cover letters, and submitting applications correctly.

Creating Professional Email Accounts

Learn to set up and manage professional email accounts for job searching and workplace communication.

Marketing & Job Search Strategies Workshop

Effective techniques for finding job opportunities, networking, and personal branding in today's digital job market.
